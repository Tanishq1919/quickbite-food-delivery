Help Centre

How long does delivery take? ▼
Most orders are delivered in 25–35 minutes. You can track your order live after placing it.
Is delivery really free? ▼
Yes, always! QuickBite charges zero delivery fees on every order, every time.
Can I order from multiple restaurants? ▼
Absolutely! Add items from different restaurants to the same cart and we'll coordinate delivery.
How do I cancel an order? ▼
Orders can be cancelled within 2 minutes of placing them. After that, please raise a complaint.
My food arrived cold / wrong item — what do I do? ▼
We're sorry! Please lodge a complaint below and our team will resolve it within 24 hours with a refund or re-delivery.
